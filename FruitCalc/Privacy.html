How FruitsCalc Works

FruitsCalc is a completely offline calculator application that:

  • Operates Locally All calculations are performed directly on your device using your device's processing power.
  • No Internet Required The app functions completely without an internet connection and never communicates with external servers.
  • Temporary Memory Only Calculation results are only stored temporarily in your device's memory while the app is active.
  • No Data Persistence When you close the app or clear the calculator, all data is immediately removed from memory.

Third-Party Services

FruitsCalc does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app is completely self-contained and does not communicate with external services.

No Third-Party Integrations

  • No Analytics We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Analytics, or any other analytics services.
  • No Advertising There are no advertisements in FruitsCalc, and we do not work with advertising networks.
  • No Social Media The app does not connect to or share data with social media platforms.
  • No Cloud Services We do not use cloud storage services or sync data across devices.

Children's Privacy

FruitsCalc is designed to be safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information from anyone, we automatically comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar international regulations.

Parents can feel confident: FruitsCalc cannot access, collect, or transmit any information about your child. The app is completely safe for children to use independently.

Data Security

While we don't collect any data, we want you to understand how your device keeps your information secure:

  • Device-Only Processing All calculations happen only on your device and never leave your device.
  • No Network Communication FruitsCalc does not have network permissions and cannot access the internet.
  • Automatic Data Clearing Any temporary calculation data is automatically cleared when you exit the app.
  • iOS Security Your data benefits from Apple's built-in iOS security features and app sandboxing.

Your Rights

  • No Account Required There are no accounts, profiles, or stored preferences to manage.
