How do I access scientific functions?
Rotate your device to landscape mode to access advanced scientific functions like trigonometry, logarithms, and more.
Why are trigonometric functions using degrees?
BlissCalculator uses degrees by default for user-friendliness. For example, sin(90) = 1, which is more intuitive for most users.
How do I clear my calculation?
Tap the "AC" (All Clear) button to reset the calculator to zero and clear any ongoing calculations.
Can I copy my results?
Tap and hold on the display to copy your calculation result to the clipboard.
Is my calculation data stored anywhere?
No! All calculations are performed locally on your device. No data is stored or transmitted to external servers.
The app isn't working correctly. What should I do?
Try force-closing and reopening the app. If problems persist, restart your device. For ongoing issues, contact us at the email above.
