üéÆHow to Use

  • Drawing: Touch and drag on the canvas to place sand particles
  • Tool Selection: Tap S1, S2, or S3 to choose different sand types
  • Walls: Select "Wall" tool to create barriers and structures
  • Text: Use "Text" tool to add words (max 10 characters)
  • Erasing: Select "Erase" tool to remove particles
  • Controls: Use play/pause button to control simulation
  • Auto Inject: Toggle automatic sand dropping from the top
  • Clear: Tap trash icon to clear the entire canvas
  • Save: Tap "Save to Photos" to capture your creation

üîßTroubleshooting

App Performance

  • Slow performance: Try clearing the canvas or pausing simulation
  • App crashes: Restart the app and avoid creating too many particles at once

Photo Saving Issues

  • Can't save photos: Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos > Falling Sand Canvas and enable access
  • Save failed: Check available storage space on your device

General Issues

  • Touch not responding: Make sure you're touching within the canvas area
  • Tools not working: Ensure you've selected the correct tool before drawing
