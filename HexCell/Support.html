Getting Started

HexCell Growth is an interactive grain growth simulation that demonstrates how crystal structures evolve in materials science. Here's how to use the app:

Start/Pause

Tap the green "Start" button to begin the simulation. The button changes to red "Pause" when running.

Add Nuclei

Tap anywhere on the black simulation area to add new crystal nucleation sites that will grow.

Adjust Settings

Use the sliders to control "Dynamics" (growth rate) and "Nuclei" (number of starting grains).

View Statistics

Tap "Stats" to see live data including cell count, filled percentage, and dynamics value.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is grain growth simulation?

A: Grain growth is a process in materials science where crystal grains in metals and other materials compete for space and merge over time. This simulation shows how hexagonal grains evolve according to scientific principles used in metallurgy research.

Q: Why does the app ask for photo library access?

A: Photo library access is only requested when you tap the "Save" button to save simulation screenshots. The app never reads your existing photos - it only adds new images when you choose to save them.

Q: The simulation seems slow or laggy. What can I do?

A: Try reducing the "Nuclei" count using the slider, or lower the "Dynamics" setting. The simulation is computationally intensive, especially on older devices.

Q: How do I clear the simulation and start over?

A: Tap the orange "Clear" button to reset the simulation to a blank state, then tap "Start" to begin a new simulation.

Q: Can I control the colors of the grains?

A: The colors are automatically generated based on scientific algorithms. Each grain gets a unique color to help distinguish between different crystal regions.

Q: Is this educationally accurate?

A: Yes! The simulation uses cellular automata algorithms based on real materials science principles. It's suitable for educational use in materials science, metallurgy, and crystallography courses.

Troubleshooting

App Performance Issues

  • Slow simulation: Reduce nuclei count and dynamics settings
  • App crashes: Try restarting the app and using lower settings
  • UI not responsive: Clear the simulation and restart

Photo Saving Issues

Permission Denied: Go to iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos > HexCell Growth and enable access.

Recommended Setting: Choose "Add Photos Only" for optimal privacy when prompted for photo access.

Common Solutions

  • Restart the app: Close and reopen HexCell Growth
  • Restart your device: A device restart can resolve memory issues
  • Update iOS: Ensure your device is running a recent iOS version
  • Free up storage: The app needs memory to run complex simulations
