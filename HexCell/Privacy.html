Privacy Policy for HexCell Growth

Effective Date: July 2, 2025

Last Updated: July 2, 2025

Quick Summary: HexCell Growth is designed with privacy in mind. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app only accesses your photo library when you choose to save simulation images.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. HexCell Growth operates entirely on your device and does not:

  • Collect personal data
  • Track user behavior
  • Use analytics services
  • Require user accounts or registration
  • Access the internet for data collection

2. Photo Library Access

HexCell Growth requests access to your photo library for the following purpose:

  • Saving Images: When you choose to save simulation screenshots, the app saves them directly to your photo library
  • User Control: This access is only used when you explicitly tap the "Save" button
  • No Reading: The app does not read or access existing photos from your library

3. Data Storage

All simulation data and app preferences are stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Simulation parameters and settings
  • App preferences
  • No data is transmitted to external servers

4. Third-Party Services

HexCell Growth does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app operates entirely offline.

5. Children's Privacy

HexCell Growth is suitable for all ages and does not collect any information from children or adults. The app is educational and safe for use by children under parental guidance.

6. Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there are no data security concerns related to external data breaches. All app data remains on your device and is protected by your device's security measures.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app update notes and on this page. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically.

8. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there are no personal data rights to exercise. You maintain full control over:

  • Photos saved to your library (managed through iOS Settings)
  • App permissions (managed through iOS Settings > Privacy & Security)
  • App data (can be deleted by uninstalling the app)

9. International Users

HexCell Growth operates the same way for all users worldwide. No personal data is collected regardless of your location.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or HexCell Growth, please contact us:

  • Email: blisssoft16@gmail.com
  • App Store: Leave a review or contact through the App Store

11. iOS Permissions Explained

Photo Library Access: When prompted by iOS, we recommend selecting "Add Photos Only" or "Selected Photos" for optimal privacy. This allows the app to save your simulation images without accessing your existing photos.

Privacy-First Design: HexCell Growth was built with privacy as a core principle. We believe educational apps should enhance learning without compromising user privacy.
