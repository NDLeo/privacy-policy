BlissLoan Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 30, 2025

Information We Collect

BlissLoan does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All loan calculations are performed locally on your device.

Data Storage

  • All calculations happen on your device
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No personal or financial information is collected
  • No user accounts or registration required

Third-Party Services

BlissLoan does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

BlissLoan does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new privacy policy in the app and updating the "effective date."

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at:
