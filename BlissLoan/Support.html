Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate my monthly payment?

Enter your loan amount, interest rate, and loan term in years. BlissLoan will instantly calculate your monthly payment.

Can I calculate different types of loans?

Yes! BlissLoan works for mortgages, auto loans, personal loans, and any fixed-rate loan.

Does the app collect my financial data?

No. All calculations are performed locally on your device. No personal or financial information is collected or transmitted.

Is BlissLoan free to use?

Yes! BlissLoan is completely free with no ads, subscriptions, or hidden fees.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! BlissLoan works completely offline. No internet connection is required.
